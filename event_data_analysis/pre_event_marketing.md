# Pre-Event Marketing Performance Analysis

## Section Objectives
The primary objective of this phase was to evaluate the efficiency of our customer acquisition funnel and promotional mechanics before the event's execution. By analyzing transaction velocity, channel performance, and discount utilization, this dashboard provides marketing stakeholders with data-driven clarity on where capital was successfully generated and where promotional strategies optimized margins.

## High-Level Performance Metrics KPIs
The top section of the dashboard features four executive scorecards providing an instant baseline of campaign health:
- **Total Sales Revenue:** Gross revenue generated across all booking channels.
- **Total Tickets Sold:** Aggregate volume of successful registrations.
- **Average Order Value AOV:** Net financial value generated per transaction.
- **Discount Conversion Rate:** The percentage of overall transactions utilizing a promotional code.

---

## Chart-by-Chart Analysis and Executive Summaries

### 1. Acquisition Channel Performance: Volume vs. Revenue
- **Chart Type:** Dual-Axis Combo Chart
- **Executive Summary:** This visual contrasts raw volume against total dollar revenue across digital networks. It surfaces channel quality by identifying high-volume platforms that generate low margins versus premium networks that drive significant financial return from fewer checkouts.

### 2. Top Revenue-Generating Promo Codes
- **Chart Type:** Ranked Performance Heatmap Table
- **Executive Summary:** This table isolates individual alphanumeric promotional codes, ranking them by gross dollar volume contributed to total ticket revenue. It explicitly highlights which affiliate partnerships, influencer hooks, or targeted corporate discount campaigns drove the heaviest bottom-line impact.

### 3. Daily Sales Revenue Trends by Channel
- **Chart Type:** Time Series Line Chart
- **Executive Summary:** A chronological timeline tracking daily revenue acquisition across marketing networks. This view maps momentum trends, campaign velocity, and the direct impact of targeted outbound marketing pushes, showing precisely when our audience transitioned from consideration to purchase.

### 4. Promo Code Redemptions by Marketing Channel
- **Chart Type:** Horizontal Bar Chart
- **Executive Summary:** This chart isolates promotional efficiency by tracking the volume of discount redemptions mapped directly to their acquisition origin. It identifies which digital ecosystems are most reactive to pricing incentives versus channels that scale organically without heavy discounting.

### 5. Promotional Campaign Velocity Over Time
- **Chart Type:** Time Series Chart
- **Executive Summary:** An operational timeline tracking when promo codes were actively redeemed across the campaign lifecycle. This helps stakeholders understand the conversion rate of marketing campaigns and identify peak discount usage windows.

## Key Analytical Insights
- **Volume vs. Value Decoupling:** Certain acquisition channels drive a disproportionately high volume of registrations but lag in gross revenue contribution, indicating a heavy reliance on lower-tier inventory or deep discounts. Conversely, a select few premium channels generate high-value checkouts with lower volume.
- **Promo Code Pareto Principle:** A small minority of active promotional codes are responsible for the vast majority of discounted revenue, pointing to highly successful target campaigns or high-impact affiliate partnerships.
- **Campaign Momentum Spikes:** Chronological tracking reveals sharp, distinct transaction spikes that directly correlate with external marketing pushes or approaching pricing deadlines, proving that purchase behavior is heavily deadline-driven.

---

## Strategic Business Recommendations
1. **Shift Ad Spend to High-AOV Channels:** Reallocate marketing budget away from high-volume, low-margin acquisition networks and reinvest in the specific digital channels proven to attract premium, full-price buyers.
2. **De-escalate Broad Discounting:** Restrict the distribution of broad, public promotional codes. Instead, duplicate the structural mechanics of the top-performing codes identified in the heatmap table to deploy highly targeted, finite affiliate campaigns.
3. **Optimize Deadline-Driven Marketing:** Schedule major marketing announcements, email drops, and ad spend bursts to hit 48 hours prior to historical velocity spikes to capitalize on natural buying momentum.
